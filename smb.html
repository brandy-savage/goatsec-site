Common questions

Questions we hear from small businesses.

I'm too small to be a target, right?

Unfortunately, no. Small businesses are targeted precisely because attackers assume they have weaker defenses. The good news: basic protections are very effective, and that's what we focus on. We're not trying to sell you a missile defense system, just solid locks on your doors.

How much does this cost?

Our small business packages are flat-fee, typically ranging from a few thousand dollars for basic assessments to modest five-figure engagements for full compliance programs. We'll give you an exact number before any work starts, no surprises, no hourly billing anxiety.

Do I need to understand technology to work with you?

Not at all. We explain everything in plain language. If you can describe how your business uses email, stores files, and handles client information, that's all we need to get started. We handle the technical details.

What if I already have an IT person or MSP?

Great, we work alongside them. IT support and security are related but different. Your IT person keeps things running; we make sure the security configuration is actually right. We'll coordinate with whoever supports your technology today.

Will this disrupt my business?

We design our work to minimize disruption. Most of what we do happens in the background. When we need your input, we schedule around your availability. Our goal is to make your business more secure without making your day harder.

Don't I just need antivirus?

Antivirus is one piece. But modern threats, phishing emails that trick your staff, compromised passwords, misconfigured cloud settings, require a broader approach. Think of it like your office: a deadbolt is great, but you also want window locks, a safe for valuables, and a plan for what to do if something goes wrong.
