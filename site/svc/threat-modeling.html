Threat Modeling

Collaborative threat modeling sessions for new features, APIs, or system designs, using STRIDE, PASTA, or a custom method. Produces a documented model your team can update and reference in design reviews.

Best for

Engineering teams building new APIs, microservices, or features with sensitive data flows. Also strong value pre-pentest to ensure test coverage aligns with real risk.

What we deliver
  • Threat model document with attack scenarios and mitigations
  • Data flow diagrams (DFD) reviewed and annotated
  • Risk-rated finding list with owner assignments
  • Template for ongoing threat model updates by your team
  • Optional: security requirements derived from the model
How we work
  • Pre-session questionnaire on architecture and trust boundaries
  • Facilitated whiteboarding session (remote or on-site)
  • STRIDE or PASTA analysis of each trust boundary crossing
  • Mitigation mapping and ownership assignment
  • Written model document with revision history

Timeline

⏱ 3–5 days
