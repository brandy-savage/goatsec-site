IAM Governance and Least-Privilege Enforcement

Audit existing IAM policies, identify over-privileged roles and service accounts, and design an enforceable least-privilege model with policy-as-code implementation.

Best for

Any cloud-heavy org where IAM has grown without governance, common after rapid team growth, acquisitions, or extended use of administrator-level service accounts.

What we deliver
  • IAM inventory: all roles, policies, and principals with risk tiers
  • Over-privilege findings with specific permission removals
  • Target IAM architecture document
  • Policy-as-code implementation (OPA, SCP, or IAM Access Analyzer)
  • Runbook for ongoing IAM hygiene reviews
How we work
  • Export and analyze all IAM policies and role assignments
  • Identify unused permissions via AWS Access Analyzer or equivalent
  • Design target least-privilege model
  • Implement enforcement via SCPs or policy-as-code
  • Validate with simulated access tests
