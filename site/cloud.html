The outcome

A cloud posture your team can maintain, not just audit.

Cloud security posture problems are almost never a tooling problem. They're a clarity problem. Teams don't know what's exposed, why IAM is messy, or which misconfigurations actually matter versus which ones are low-risk noise. We cut through that.

After a cloud security engagement with GoatSec, you have a prioritized view of your actual risk surface, a documented IAM governance model your team can enforce, and hardening runbooks that don't require us to maintain them.

When to use this offering
  • ✦ Your cloud environment has grown faster than your security controls
  • ✦ IAM is sprawling and you have no documented least-privilege model

Methodology

We use the CIS Benchmarks and CSA Cloud Controls Matrix as our assessment framework, adapted to your specific cloud provider and services. For multi-cloud environments, we prioritize the highest-risk surface first rather than trying to review everything at once.

IAM review follows a least-privilege analysis model: we map every role, policy, and cross-account trust relationship, then flag overprivileged access, unused credentials, and paths that allow privilege escalation.

Inputs we need from you

  • ✦ Existing IaC repositories if available
  • ✦ Any existing audit or scan results from prior work
  • ✦ Architecture diagram or verbal walkthrough from your platform team
