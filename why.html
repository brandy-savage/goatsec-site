How we work

White-glove delivery meets technical depth.

We're not a staffing agency, a vendor, or a consulting firm in the traditional sense. We're the partner who takes a security problem end-to-end and produces something concrete on the other side.

1
Problem-first scoping

We start from your problem statement, not a product catalog. Every engagement is scoped to what actually needs to happen, with defined inputs, outputs, and a timeline you sign off on before any work begins.

2
Vendor intermediary model

When tools or vendors are involved, we run that relationship. We sit in the vendor meetings, push feature priorities, negotiate renewals, and implement configurations. You get a briefing, not a full-time job.

3
Continuous, structured communication

Weekly updates with a consistent format. Blockers surfaced proactively, not after they become your problem. Async channels available so you're never out of the loop without having to ask.

4
Artifacts that outlast the engagement

Reports, runbooks, board summaries, integration configs, and policy documentation. Everything is handed off in a form your team can operate without us. Engagements end. Artifacts don't.
