The problem we solve

Most vuln management programs have the same three problems.

Too many findings with no logic for deciding which ones matter. Remediation SLAs written in policy documents that nobody actually tracks. And dashboards that report vulnerability counts without connecting them to business risk. We fix the program, not just the scanner output.

  • ✦ You have remediation SLAs in your policy but no automated tracking for them
  • ✦ Engineering teams push back on findings because the severity ratings feel unreliable
  • ✦ Your executive dashboard shows vulnerability count, not risk reduction velocity
  • ✦ You've recently grown through acquisition and have inherited multiple scanning programs with no coherent view
  • ✦ Your CTEM or exposure management initiative needs operational design, not just a slide deck

Methodology

We assess program maturity against a model informed by NIST CSF, CISA BOD directives, and practical operational benchmarks. Prioritization design uses EPSS (Exploit Prediction Scoring System) alongside CVSS to surface findings that are actually being exploited in the wild, not just theoretically severe.

Remediation workflows are designed around your actual team structure and ticketing system, not a generic template. We integrate with what you already use.

Inputs we need from you
  • ✦ Current scanning tools and sample output
  • ✦ Existing remediation policy or SLA documentation
  • ✦ Asset inventory or asset classification approach
  • ✦ Ticketing system and CI/CD toolchain access for integration
