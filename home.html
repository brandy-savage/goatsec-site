How it works

Scope. Execute. Prove.

We keep it simple because simple is what actually ships. Every engagement follows three phases, and you know exactly what happens in each one before we start.

1
Make the outcome explicit

Before any work begins, we write down the problem, the constraints, what done looks like, and how long it takes. You sign off. That becomes the SOW. No ambiguity, no scope creep by default.

2
Do the work, unblock fast

Hands-on execution with weekly structured updates. We handle vendor friction, integration snags, and technical decisions as they come up, not as escalations for you to untangle.

3
Leave behind things that last

Every engagement closes with evidence-grade artifacts: reports your board can read, runbooks your team can follow, and integration configs that don't require us to maintain them.
